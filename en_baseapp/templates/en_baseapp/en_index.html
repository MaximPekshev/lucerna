Granulated straw

Straw granules for bedding in Horse stalls. What are the advantages of straw granules and why are they inferior to the usual litter for stalls according to several criteria? Unlike sawdust, straw granules absorb twice as much moisture and perfectly keep the smell of ammonia, and waste from such a litter is an excellent fertilizer, unlike extremely slowly decomposing sawdust. In addition, the latter may contain chemicals that were used to treat trees, which will definitely not have a positive effect on the health of the horse.

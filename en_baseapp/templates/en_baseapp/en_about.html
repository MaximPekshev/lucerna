About us

Our company was established relatively recently.

Date of foundation - 03.03.2020.

The experience and enthusiasm of our employees allows us to develop at a fairly rapid pace. In May 2020, a pelletizing production line with a capacity of 100 tons per day was put into operation, which allows providing most of the leading feed mills in Russia, as well as plants and farms in Europe with raw materials.

Thanks to a well-chosen staff, a modern, technological production process worked out to the smallest detail, the quality of products, as well as love for our work, we strive to ensure the health of animals, maximum genetically inherent productivity (dairy, egg, meat – live weight gain), reduce morbidity and mortality, reduce the cost of treating livestock, increase the immune status and resistance to infections, and, as a result, increase the economic efficiency of farming.

We are aware of our responsibility

Products are delivered to our consumers naturally, high quality and environmentally friendly. We are aware of our responsibility in the manufacture of healthy feed, bedding and litters that meet the highest requirements. The release of flawless products is the main task of the company today, this allows us to look confidently into the future.

We are sure that our products will interest the widest circle of people, from children who want to treat their pet with a crispy, fragrant “tasty treat”, owners of personal subsidiary farms, rabbit breeders who care about the safety and health of their wards, to equestrian sports schools, large fish farms and agricultural holdings using our alfalfa granule for full-ration fattening, and straw granule for antiseptic absorbent bedding for growing poultry, cattle, pigs.
